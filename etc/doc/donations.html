Simple Log - Donations

As simple as it is, I have spent a lot of time on Simple Log. Designing a simple interface, writing the code, getting it just right, investigating strange anomalies, staying up late to fix bugs, and writing documentation for you entertainment; these are all tasks that take a significant amount of time - my spare time.

In essence, I've donated my time to you.

If you find that you save time or money by using Simple Log, I'd really appreciate it if you would consider saying thank you by giving me some money.

Donations are accepted online through the secure PayPal service. PayPal allows you to make secure payments over the internet without disclosing your bank or credit card details to the person being paid. You do not need a PayPal account to make a donation.
